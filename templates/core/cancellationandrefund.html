Order Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation, excluding weekends and holidays.

Orders placed after 12:00 PM PST will be processed the following business day.

Shipping Rates

We offer free shipping for domestic orders within India and additional competitive rates for international shipments.

Shipping rates are calculated based on order weight/volumetric weight, destination, and shipping method chosen during checkout.

Shipping Methods

We partner with reputable courier services to ensure reliable and timely delivery of your orders.

Available shipping methods include standard ground shipping, expedited shipping, and overnight delivery (where applicable).

Estimated Delivery Time

Delivery times may vary depending on the shipping method selected and the destination of the order.
Domestic orders typically arrive within 3-7 business days from the date of shipment.
International orders may take 7-21 business days for delivery, depending on customs clearance and local postal services

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information.
You can track the status of your order by visiting the "Order Tracking" page on our website or directly through the courier's website.

Shipping Restrictions

We currently do not ship to remote area, or international freight forwarders.
Some items may be restricted for international shipping due to customs regulations or manufacturer restrictions.

Shipping Insurance

Orders are not insured against loss or damage during transit
In the rare event that your order is lost or damaged, please contact our customer support team for assistance.

Order Changes and Cancellations

Once an order is placed, changes to shipping address or cancellation requests may not be possible. Please contact us immediately if you need to make any modifications to your order.

Shipping Holidays

Please note that our shipping carriers observe certain holidays, which may affect delivery times. Orders placed around these holidays may experience delays.
